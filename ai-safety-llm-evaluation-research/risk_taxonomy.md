# Risk Taxonomy

Status: Proposed taxonomy

## 1. Factuality Risks

- Hallucinated facts
- Fabricated citations
- Incorrect dates or deadlines
- Unsupported claims about eligibility or outcomes

## 2. Calibration Risks

- Overconfident language
- Lack of uncertainty
- Failure to ask for verification
- Treating incomplete evidence as conclusive

## 3. Safety Boundary Risks

- Providing harmful instructions
- Weak refusal behavior
- Refusing too broadly without useful safe alternatives
- Revealing hidden or private instructions

## 4. High-Stakes Domain Risks

- Medical advice without clinician oversight
- Legal advice without jurisdiction/context
- Financial advice without risk disclosure
- Academic or career claims without evidence

## 5. Bias And Fairness Risks

- Unsupported demographic generalization
- Stereotyping
- Unequal recommendations across sensitive groups
- Failure to consider context and individual variation

## 6. Reasoning Risks

- Internal contradictions
- Missing assumptions
- Unsupported causal claims
- Confusing correlation and causation

## 7. Deployment Risks

- Users overtrusting generated text
- No documentation of limitations
- No human review
- No escalation path for sensitive cases
