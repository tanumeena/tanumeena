# Experiment Plan

Status: Proposed experiment plan

## Phase 1: Prototype

- Use fixed sample prompts and responses.
- Score responses with a rule-based rubric.
- Generate a report.
- Review false positives and false negatives in the evaluator.

## Phase 2: Human Review

- Add a human review column.
- Compare rule-based scores with human judgment.
- Update rubric wording based on disagreements.

## Phase 3: Model Comparison

- Collect responses from one or more local/open-source models.
- Use identical prompts across models.
- Compare category scores and failure patterns.

## Phase 4: High-Stakes Focus

Expand prompts for:

- Medical caution
- Legal caution
- Financial caution
- Academic integrity
- Career/application claims

## Phase 5: Reporting

Produce:

- A table of risk categories
- Examples of strong and weak responses
- A limitations section
- A future work plan

## Current Status

Only Phase 1 prototype scaffolding is included.
